Plasma Derived Products

Darman Ara was co-founder of five Plasmapheresis centers during 2008 to 2011, in addition two centers have been founded by Darman Ara independently. There are fourteen plasma collection centers up and running by the end of 2022. In 2023, there are nine new plasma centers under construction from which Darman Ara Group is founder of six.

Infusion Therapy

The field of infusion therapy implies the treatment of a patient with intravenously applied infusion solutions in order to enable a patient’s recovery or improvement of his illness. Depending on the underlying illness, the solution contains an appropriate active pharmaceutical ingredient.
